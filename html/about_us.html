About us

GRESSO DRONE is part of the Gresso Group, which was trademarked in 1999 and has been doing business in North America since 2011. With the growing need for security in the both the public and private sector, Gresso was decided to launch a military-grade anti-drone Technology in the US Market. This state-of-the-art systems have been implemented successfully in Europe for over 15 years with commercial enterprise and government partners.
GRESSO DRONE aims to deliver enterprise-level anti-drone solutions in both the commercial, government and private sectors. Our military-grade radar technology is paired with location mapping software and an advanced jamming product for comprehensive drone security. Unlike our competitors, our systems are purposefully designed to be hassle-free and user-friendly, without compromising the quality of protection.
